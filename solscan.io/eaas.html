SVM Explorer as a Service (EaaS)
Solscan's Explorer-as-a-Service (EAAS) provides consultation for blockchain projects and a comprehensive solution for deploying a fully hosted and managed block explorer. By leveraging Solscan's proven infrastructure, this service ensures seamless integration, real-time visibility of blockchain data, and smart contract verification capabilities.
Solscan's EAAS is ideal for projects seeking a robust, user-friendly blockchain explorer without the complications of in-house development.
